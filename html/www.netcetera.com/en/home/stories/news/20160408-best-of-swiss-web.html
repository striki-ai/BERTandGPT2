Two Bronze awards at Best of Swiss Web

IT-dreamjobs and Ticketcorner ski portal scoop prizes

Two of our projects won prizes at the Best of Swiss Web Award night on 7 April.

The website IT-dreamjobs.ch shows the fascinating variety of jobs and industries in which software engineers work. It received a Bronze award in the category Public Affairs. Netcetera’s apprentices programmed the website. The Ticketcorner ski portal, developed by Netcetera, received Bronze in the category Business Efficiency. On this platform, snow sports enthusiasts can get the latest information and their ski tickets anywhere and anytime – without waiting at the checkout.
