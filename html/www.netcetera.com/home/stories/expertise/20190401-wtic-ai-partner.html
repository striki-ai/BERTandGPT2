Are machines interested in learning the insurance business?

Under the moto “Reinforcing customer engagement & Operational agility through digital transformation”, the 14th World Takaful & InsurTech Conference takes place on 1 - 2 April in Dubai. Under the moto “Reinforcing customer engagement & Operational agility through digital transformation”, the 14th World Takaful & InsurTech Conference will takes place on 1 - 2 April 2019 in Dubai. WTIC 2019 is the definitive ultimate forum to meet leading minds from Takaful, InsurTech and Insurance Innovation, with representation from over 35 countries, ranging from key markets for Takaful such as the GCC and South East Asia to emerging markets such as Africa and Europe.

As a company addressing GCC specific healthcare insurance issues, we have joined as an exclusive artificial intelligence partner. Our Managing Director on the Middle East, Kiril Milev, will present the experience and logic behind our fraudulent pattern detection software solution, RiSIC. As a positive use case, RiSIC is addressing waste, fraud and abuse in the health insurance industry based on historical transactions, qualifying the anomaly of each new transaction compared to the history and assign a potential risk to it.

You can learn more about our product at risic.ae
